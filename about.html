About SafeSignal

Building emergency technology with safety, consent, and trust

SafeSignal is a public-interest technology project focused on helping people ask for help quickly while protecting privacy and user choice.

Project Purpose

The purpose of SafeSignal is to create a faster, simpler way for people to send emergency alerts when they feel unsafe or experience a health crisis. The project centers vulnerable situations where a person may not be able to make a normal phone call.

Public Interest Technology Guidelines

We incorporated Public Interest Technology Guidelines by focusing on safety, accessibility, privacy, consent, equity, and accountability. The solution supports vulnerable groups and allows users to control trusted contacts, location sharing, and alert settings. Nearby helpers must consent before receiving alerts.

Challenges

  • Avoiding false alarms
  • Protecting user privacy
  • Designing for panic situations
  • Connecting different support groups
  • Making the app accessible for everyone

What We Learned

We learned that emergency technology must be simple, fast, and human-centered. AI can support decision-making, but it must be used responsibly with privacy, consent, and trust.
