Project overview

Emergency alerts powered by human-centered AI

SafeSignal combines one-tap alerts, voice triggers, signal analysis, and trusted response networks to help people get support faster.

Problem Statement

Every day, people, especially women, children, elderly individuals, young adults, commuters, and people with health conditions, may face unsafe situations or sudden medical emergencies. During panic or health crises, they may freeze, forget to call for help, or be unable to use their phone.

Solution Overview

SafeSignal is an AI-powered emergency signal app that allows users to send instant distress alerts through one tap, voice command, or automatic AI detection. Alerts can be sent to nearby helpers, trusted contacts, and local authorities or emergency services based on the danger level.

How the app works

A clear flow from risk to response

  1. 1User feels unsafe or experiences emergency
  2. 2App detects danger through one tap, voice command, or AI signals
  3. 3App classifies the danger level
  4. 4Alert is sent to trusted contacts, nearby helpers, and emergency support
  5. 5User’s live location and emergency status are shared

AI/Tech Integration

AI and technology make emergency response faster, smarter, and more automatic. The app can detect danger or health emergencies through abnormal heart rate, emergency voice keywords, panic in the user’s voice, sudden movement, falling, inactivity, and live location changes. User journey

Designed around real emergency needs

Night commute

An NYC resident walking alone at night needs a fast emergency signal app to alert nearby helpers, trusted contacts, and local authorities in order to get immediate help when they feel unsafe.

Health emergency

A person with a health condition needs an AI-powered emergency app that can detect warning signs and automatically send alerts in order to get help when they cannot call for assistance.

Trusted response

A trusted contact or nearby helper needs to receive the user’s live location, danger level, and emergency status in order to respond quickly and coordinate support.
